Landlord Premium — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

The short version

We collect nothing. Landlord Premium has no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no tracking. Everything you put into the app — leases, tenant details, documents, financial records — is stored in a private database on your device.

Where your data lives

All app data is stored locally on your Mac or iPhone. Documents you attach (leases, credit reports, bank statements, insurance certificates) are read on your device to fill in fields; their contents are never transmitted anywhere.

On-device AI

The app's document reading and search run entirely on your device. The optional "accurate AI" is a one-time download of a language model (about 4 GB) from a public model repository; after download it works fully offline. No document content is ever sent to any AI service.

iCloud sync (optional, off by default)

If you switch on iCloud sync, your data syncs through your own iCloud account using Apple's private CloudKit database, encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple and tied to your Apple ID. We have no access to it. Turning sync off keeps all data solely on your device.

Apple Reminders

If you grant Reminders access, the app creates reminders in a "Landlord" list in Apple Reminders so they can alert you on your devices. This uses Apple's frameworks on-device; no reminder content is sent to us.

What we do NOT do

  • No collection of personal data, usage data, or diagnostics
  • No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
  • No accounts, no sign-in, no server of ours
  • No selling or sharing of any data — we never see it

Your tenants' data

Documents you store about tenants (IDs, credit reports, bank statements) remain on your device under your control. Deleting a document in the app removes it. You are responsible for handling your tenants' information in line with your local laws.
